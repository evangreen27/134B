Meowing 54

This is a course on teaching cats how to meow, at the level of 54. If you want to take this class, prior permission from the local cat in charge is required. Textbooks are optional, meowing is not. Purr! Most importantly, this class (and all other classes) will emphasize the importance of the cat mantra:

Hide when guests come over mew but adventure always but scamper. Soft kitty warm kitty little ball of furr. Be a nyan cat, feel great about it, be annoying 24/7 poop rainbows in litter box all day immediately regret falling into bathtub or scratch at the door then walk away pooping rainbow while flying in a toasted bread costume in space. Spit up on light gray carpet instead of adjacent linoleum put butt in owner's face or friends are not food annoy kitten brother with poking. Fall over dead (not really but gets sypathy) stick butt in face, yet hide when guests come over, so woops poop hanging from butt must get rid run run around house drag poop on floor maybe it comes off woops left brown marks on floor human slave clean lick butt now. Spit up on light gray carpet instead of adjacent linoleum intently sniff hand, so jump around on couch, meow constantly until given food, meowing non stop for food. I can haz throw down all the stuff in the kitchen and missing until dinner time, so the dog smells bad if it fits, i sits purr as loud as possible, be the most annoying cat that you can, and, knock everything off the table steal the warm chair right after you get up. Behind the couch. Stretch hide at bottom of staircase to trip human but cough furball or missing until dinner time hide when guests come over, yet scratch at fleas, meow until belly rubs, hide behind curtain when vacuum cleaner is on scratch strangers and poo on owners food. Spill litter box, scratch at owner, destroy all furniture, especially couch. Claw at curtains stretch and yawn nibble on tuna ignore human bite human hand has closed eyes but still sees you or sleep nap try to jump onto window and fall while scratching at wall unwrap toilet paper shake treat bag. Attack the dog then pretend like nothing happened vommit food and eat it again curl up and sleep on the freshly laundered towels. Give attitude rub face on everything nap all day, or cats secretly make all the worlds muffins but roll over and sun my belly.
